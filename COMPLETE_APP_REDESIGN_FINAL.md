## 🎨 Unified Design System

### Consistent Across All Pages

**Background:**
- Medical gradient (#E3F2FD → #E8F5E9)
- Soft, calming blue-green transition
- Professional medical aesthetic

**Cards:**
- Corner radius: 16-24dp
- Elevation: 2-12dp
- White background
- Generous padding: 20-28dp

**Icons:**
- Sizes: 28-140dp
- Circular backgrounds with 15% opacity
- Color-coded by function
- Elevated icon cards on auth pages

**Typography:**
- Titles: 20-32sp, bold, sans-serif-medium
- Values: 40-64sp, bold, sans-serif-light
- Labels: 13-16sp, regular, sans-serif
- Hints: 11-12sp, regular, sans-serif

**Buttons:**
- Height: 60-64dp
- Corner radius: 16-20dp
- Text: 16-18sp, medium
- Icons: 24-28dp
- No caps text

**Input Fields:**
- Corner radius: 12-16dp
- Start icons for context
- End icons for actions
- 18-20dp vertical padding
- Primary color on focus

**Colors:**
- Primary: #2196F3 (Medical Blue)
- Heart Rate: #E91E63 (Pink/Red)
- SpO2: #2196F3 (Blue)
- Temperature: #FF9800 (Orange)
- Status Good: #4CAF50 (Green)
- Status Warning: #FFB300 (Amber)
- Status Critical: #F44336 (Red)

## 🎯 Design Principles Applied

### 1. Material You (Material Design 3)
- ✅ Dynamic color system
- ✅ Large corner radius (16-24dp)
- ✅ Elevated cards with shadows
- ✅ Generous spacing and padding
- ✅ Modern, clean aesthetic
- ✅ Smooth animations

### 2. Medical UI Best Practices
- ✅ High contrast (WCAG AA compliant)
- ✅ Large readable numbers (40-64sp)
- ✅ Color coding (green/yellow/red)
- ✅ Professional appearance
- ✅ Trustworthy design
- ✅ Clear visual hierarchy

### 3. Child-Friendly Design
- ✅ Soft rounded corners
- ✅ Friendly icons (baby face)
- ✅ Calming gradient backgrounds
- ✅ Warm color palette
- ✅ Approachable but professional
- ✅ Not childish, but welcoming

### 4. Accessibility
- ✅ WCAG AA compliant colors
- ✅ Large touch targets (48dp+)
- ✅ Content descriptions on all icons
- ✅ High contrast text
- ✅ Screen reader friendly
- ✅ Clear visual hierarchy
